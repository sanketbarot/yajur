SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) is a method of investing a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds. It helps in building wealth over time through compounding and rupee cost averaging. You can start with as low as ₹500/month.

The ideal investment depends on your goals, income, and expenses. Try to invest at least 20-30% of your income. Our advisors can help you determine the right amount.

Mutual funds are regulated by SEBI and managed by professional fund managers. While all investments carry some risk, diversification reduces it. Long-term SIP investments have historically given good returns.
